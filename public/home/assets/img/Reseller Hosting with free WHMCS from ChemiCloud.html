Reseller Hosting FAQs

What is reseller hosting?

Reseller web hosting is an opportunity to sell and provide web hosting services to your own customers. It usually involves a hosting provider that rents hard drive space and bandwidth to your business. Then, you rent that space to your customers. Its a great way for web developers and designers to offer additional services to their clients.

How does reseller hosting work?

If youre interested in reseller hosting, youll need to select a hosting company that suits your needs and purchase a reseller package. Youll need to understand your target market to locate new customers. Then, youll create branded hosting packages to sell to your customers, choose your price, and start selling. Finally, sit back and let the money add up in your bank account.

How do I make money with reseller hosting?

The easiest way to get started earning money with reseller hosting is to find a target niche and audience to sell your hosting services to. When you understand what your customers need from a hosting provider, you can customize the packages and plans youll offer them. With ChemiCloud, youll get WHMCS to automate all of your client management and billing needs.

Is overselling allowed?

Yes! You can create packages and accounts that exceed your account's limits. If you arent familiar with this, overselling means youre able to sell more resources (disk space/bandwidth) than you have allocated to your reseller account.

Whats the difference between your affiliate program and reseller hosting?

With reseller hosting, youll be selling web hosting services to your customers under your own brand name. ChemiClouds web hosting affiliate program works by referring clients from your website to ours. When they sign up through your referral link, youll earn a commission of up to $125 per sign up. Reseller hosting provides more services to your clients and allows you to earn an ongoing income.
